# Out Of Office 🏖️

**Private mutual interest signaling for the workplace.**

*When "let's grab coffee" means something more.*

---

## What is OOO?

Out Of Office lets you privately signal romantic interest in someone from work — without the risk of rejection or HR drama.

**How it works:**
1. Stake $10 to signal interest in someone (via LinkedIn)
2. Your signal is **completely private** — encrypted on Aztec Network
3. If they signal you back → **you both find out**
4. If they don't → **nobody ever knows**

No awkward conversations. No career risk. Just cryptographic discretion.

---

## Why?

Office romance is a minefield:
- **Power dynamics** make it risky to express interest
- **Rejection** can make work unbearable
- **HR policies** discourage any signal at all
- **Reputation** is on the line

OOO solves this with **zero-knowledge privacy**. Your interest is mathematically hidden unless it's mutual.

- **Identity**: LinkedIn profiles
